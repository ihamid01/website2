Adoption

The Medford Animal Shelter is commited to matching animals to their forever home. Our adoption process is designed to ensure both the animal and adopter are compatible.

We have animals of every breed, size, and age. Contact us for more information.

All of the following are included in the adoption fees:
  • Vaccinations
  • Spay and Neuter services
  • Microchip registration
  • Health screening and veternary examinations
  • Flee, tick, and heartworm treatments
  • A starter bag of food for dogs and cats
  • And More!

    • Fostering

    The Medford Animal Shelter is looking for patient and responsible individuals to open up their homes to animals in need. Taking an animal out of our adoption center creates room for other animals. You can help us find more pets loving homes by fostering today. The length of commitment varies from a week to a few months. To learn more about fostering, give us a call!
